Lee Know's Profile

Lee Know (Stray Kids) Profile and Facts:

- He was born in Gimpo, South Korea.
- He is an only child.
- Education: Gimpo Jeil Technical High School (Graduated)
- Minho has three cats: Soon-ie, Doong-ie, Dori.
- His favorite color is mint.
- Minho has a scar on his tummy from a surgery he had as a kid.
- His favorite dance genre is hip-hop.
- His hobbies are hiking, choreographing, and watching movies.
- His frequent habit is cracking his fingers.
- Minho has fear of heights.
- He described himself as a “cute person who eats well”.
- Minho can't swim.
- Minho is ambidextrous.
- Minho doesn't have a strict skin care routine, he says he just works out, eats well and doesn't eat after 6pm.
- Lee Know said that when he dances he doesn't want to look at the mirror because it distracts him.
- When he was in elementary school, Minho wanted to be a policeman.
- He was eliminated on November 7, episode 4 of Mnet's Stray Kids but he was added back at the end of episode 9.
- His role model is 2PM's Taecyeon.
- Minho made the choreo for “Hellevator“.
- His motto: “Let's eat well, and live well.”
- Lee Know's ideal type: A person who matches well with him and someone he can talk comfortably with.
